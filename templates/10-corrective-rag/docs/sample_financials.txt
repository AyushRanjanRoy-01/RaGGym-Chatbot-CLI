GlobalBank Holdings — Q2 2024 Interim Results

Revenue: $7.34B, +8% YoY. Net Interest Income $4.1B; Non-Interest Income $3.24B.
Pre-Tax Profit: $2.08B, up 14% from $1.82B in Q2 2023.
Return on Equity (ROE): 16.2% vs 14.8% prior year.
Cost-to-Income Ratio: 52.3%, improved from 55.1% YoY.

Capital & Liquidity:
  - CET1 Capital Ratio: 14.7% (well above regulatory requirement of 4.5%).
  - Tier 1 Leverage Ratio: 6.1%.
  - Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR): 138%.

Credit Quality:
  - Non-Performing Loan (NPL) Ratio: 1.1%, slightly above 0.9% in Q2 2023.
  - Loan Loss Provisions: $420M (+18% YoY) driven by commercial real estate exposure.
  - Net Charge-Off Rate: 0.42%.

Business Segments:
  - Retail Banking: Revenue $3.1B; mortgage originations down 22% on higher rates.
  - Corporate Banking: Revenue $2.6B; trade finance volumes +9%.
  - Investment Banking: Revenue $1.64B; IPO pipeline recovering, DCM volumes strong.

Outlook:
  FY2024 ROE guidance raised to 15.5–16.5%.
  Management cautious on CRE sector; additional provisions likely in H2.
  Digital transformation investment $800M for FY2024; branch count reduced by 120.
